Children's Choir

About the Choir

Our children's choir program provides a nurturing environment where young singers develop vocal technique, musicianship, and confidence. Students learn to read music, sing in harmony, and perform as an ensemble. Space is very limited as this is a smaller choir, so please inquire today! Cottonwood students are welcome as well as independent parties.

Program Details

  • Ages: 8-12 years old
  • Schedule: TBA (1 1-hr class per week)
  • Location: Rancho Cordova
  • Tuition: $665/term per child (discount available for multiple children)
  • Performance Opportunities: Winter and Spring concerts

What to Expect

Students will learn proper vocal technique, music reading skills, and ensemble singing. No prior experience necessary!

Music Fundamentals

About the Choir

Children will learn the basics of rhythm, notation, and reading music. In addition, students will develop their ear to recognize notes in a scale and basic intervals. Space is very limited as this is a smaller choir, so please inquire today! Cottonwood students are welcome as well as independent parties.

Program Details

  • Ages: 8-12 years old
  • Schedule: TBA (1 1-hr class per week)
  • Location: Rancho Cordova
